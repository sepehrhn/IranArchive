# Submitting a Campaign

The Iran Archive Campaigns section helps amplify petitions and collective actions. We welcome submissions from the community.

## How to Submit

### Option 2: Email

Identify the required details (see checklist below) and email them to `contact@iranarchive.net`.

## Submission Checklist

Please ensure you provide at least:
-   **Campaign Title**
-   **URL** (e.g., Change.org link)
-   **Summary** (Brief description of the goal)
-   **Target Audience/Region** (e.g., Global, G7, specific country)
-   **Organizer/Initiator** (if public)
