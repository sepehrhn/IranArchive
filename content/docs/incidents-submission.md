# Submitting an Incident

The Iran Archive Incidents database tracks human rights violations and other significant events. Submissions are rigorously reviewed.

## How to Submit

### Option 1: Tag us on ğ• (Twitter)

Tag our ğ• page [@Iran_Archive](https://x.com/Iran_Archive) with details of the incident.

### Option 2: Email

Identify the required details (see checklist below) and email them to `contact@iranarchive.net`.

## Submission Checklist

Please ensure you provide at least:
-   **Date & Time** of the incident
-   **Location** (Coordinates, Street, City)
-   **Description** of what happened
-   **Evidence** (Photos, Videos, Links to reports)
-   **Source** (Who is reporting this?)

## Verification Policy

We employ a strict verification process. Incidents are classified as:
-   **Verified**: Confroimed by multiple independent sources or trusted partners.
-   **Not Verified**: Included in the database but lacks independent confirmation.

All disputed or unverified claims should be clearly marked.
