# Submitting a Victim Record

The Iran Archive Victims database documents those who have suffered under the regime. We strive for accuracy and welcome community submissions to help complete the record.

## How to Submit

### Option 1: Tag us on ğ• (Twitter)

Tag our ğ• page [@Iran_Archive](https://x.com/Iran_Archive) with details of the victim.

### Option 2: Email

Identify the required details (see checklist below) and email them to `contact@iranarchive.net`.

## Submission Checklist

Please ensure you provide at least:
-   **Full Name** (in English and Persian if possible)
-   **Date of Incident/Arrest/Death**
-   **Location** (City, Province)
-   **Description of Events**
-   **Verification Source** (Link to news report, social media post, or obituary)
-   **Photo** (if available)

## Sensitivity Notice

We prioritize the safety of victims and their families. If you are submitting sensitive information, please assume a "Not Verified" status until we can independently corroborate the details.
